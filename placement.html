3MTT: A Global-Standard Hybrid Program for future Tech Leaders

Our curriculum, delivered by top online platforms and in-person sessions at local training institutes, provides world-class education.

Graduates of 3MTT Program are skilled and ready for internships, full-time tech jobs, and freelance MicroJobs. Discover and leverage our tech talents to elevate your organization.

Why you should hire a 3MTT Fellow

Job Readiness

Through the rigorous 3MTT technical and non-technical training, we produce highly curious and career-ready fellows who have been equipped with the requisite skills to take up internships and full time jobs.

Build a Solid Talent Pipeline

We provide access to a young, vibrant and malleable talent pipeline that can help you build your dream team ensuring that your company culture is upheld.

Cost-effective Services

Employers, especially those in tech-enabled sectors, are invited to hire talent at fair and reasonable industry rates within the Nigerian market.

Spread and Reach

3MTT fellows are spread across all 36 states plus the Federal Capital Territory (FCT). They are fluent in multiple indigenous languages within Nigeria, making them well-positioned to carry out data collection and research work in hard-to reach areas.

Experienced hires

Our talent pool includes skilled tech individuals with proven work experience across various industries, ready to step into mid-levels roles within your organization.

Corporate Social Responsibility

By providing valuable work experience to 3MTT Fellows, you will be contributing your quota to nation-building, and supporting our vision of building Nigeria’s technical talent backbone.
