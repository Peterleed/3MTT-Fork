Connecting technical talents with a network of partners to empower the future workforce of Nigeria’s digital economy.

The 3 Million Technical Talent (3MTT) programme is dedicated to building Nigeria’s technical talent backbone. By partnering with us, you can contribute to shaping the future of technology and positioning of Nigeria as a global leader in technical talent.

Our Current Partners

The 3MTT programme is supported by leading organisations that are committed to fostering the growth of technical talent in Nigeria

How to Partner with Us

We invite you to be part of this transformative journey. Here’s how you can get involved:

Placements

Offer internships or employment opportunities to our talented fellows.

Benefits & Perks

Offer your products, discounts, and exclusive access to our community of 1.7 million learners. Support their learning journey while showcasing your products to future industry leaders.

MicroJobs

Leverage our fellows across every LGA in the country for short-term projects.

Sponsorship & Donations

Support our hackathons, innovation challenges, learning licenses, knowledge showcases, and other program components through contributions.

Research Collaborations

Partner with us on research projects that align with your organisational goals and leverage the innovative ideas of our fellows.

Business Process Outsourcing

Scale your operations by hiring from our extensive pool of skilled talent.

Why Partner with 3MTT?

Partnering with 3MTT offers numerous benefits

Access to a Highly Skilled Talent Pool

Unlock early access to Nigeria’s top-tier talent, trained in software development, UI/UX design, data science, AI/machine learning, and other cutting-edge fields

Brand Exposure

Extend your brand’s visibility and reputation by partnering with a national program committed to technological innovation and workforce development

Early Access to Innovative Solutions

Benefit from early access to innovative projects and solutions developed by our talented fellows, which can be integrated into your business operations.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Alignment

Showcase your commitment to national development and social impact by supporting a program that empowers the next generation of tech leaders
